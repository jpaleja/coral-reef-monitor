To help users evaluate and navigate the data layers further than the initial design, functionality was added to provide a pop-up summary of all layers on a clicked point and a distance measuring tool to help identify different reefs and measure differences between them and their locations. The dropdown search and zoom in function was removed to generate different functionality. Marine mammal data from the International Union of Conservation of Nature and Natural Resources (IUCN) and EEZ polygons outlining the territory boundaries, clipped to the pilot East Africa area, were added to the map to provide additional data to test within the summary pop-up. However, with the marine mammals data, only the top most species appears in the pop-up due to the overlapping polygons (species ranges) in the same layer and although an ideal scenario could be where a list of the species in the clicked point are displayed, this functionality could not be developed in time. Additionally, the distance measuring function was not smooth and the calculation, generated below the map, was not accurate.
